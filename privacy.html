Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Mac Games ("we", "us", or "our") operates the macgames.me website and the Chaos Path mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data when you use our services:

  • Google Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo.
  • Review profile: Your Google display name and profile photo may be shown with game reviews.
  • Game reviews: Ratings and messages you submit for games are stored in our database.
  • Device & usage data (mobile app): The Chaos Path app may collect anonymous usage data such as crash reports and session length via third-party SDKs (Unity Analytics, Google AdMob).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your rating and message below the related game.
  • To identify you as a registered user across sessions.
  • To improve the app and website experience.
  • To show relevant advertisements in the mobile app via Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data independently:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Mobile advertising. Google Privacy Policy
  • Unity Analytics: App usage analytics. Unity Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

Game reviews and user profile references are stored as long as your account exists or until deletion is requested. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@macgames.me.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact us at contact@macgames.me.
